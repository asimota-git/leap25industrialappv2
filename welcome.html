Challenge

In Industrial Production, the flotation process is important, but maintaining the onspec pH levels is challenging. pH sensors often stop working due to solid buildup, making real-time monitoring unreliable. This leads to long delays in quality checks, causing significant losses due to waste and inefficiencies....

Solution

KPMG developed an intelligent application that predicts production metrics using real-time data. The app features a simple, visual interface for decision-makers and includes an AI-powered chatbot for easy communication. Built with Microsoft technologies, it turns complex data into actionable insights....

Impact

This innovation has improved efficiency, reduced waste, and raised production standards. More than just a predictive tool, it empowers decision-makers with real-time insights, transforming the way mining operations are managed....
